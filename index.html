Hi! I'm a PhD student in the Psychology Department at the University of Pennsylvania. My advisor is John Trueswell and I'm a member of his Language Learning Lab. My research focuses on how language learners can use distributional learning in tandem with a small vocabulary of known words to acquire syntax-to-semantics relationships.

Before beginning grad school, I worked with Richard Aslin as a lab manager for two years in the Language, Learning, and Multisensory Brain (LLAMB) Lab at the Yale Child Study Center. I earned my BA in Psychology from the University of Pennsylvania in 2021.
